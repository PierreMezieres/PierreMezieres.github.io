Pierre Mézières

I am a Computer Science PhD student at the University Paul Sabatier III of Toulouse, supervised by Mathias Paulin, head of the STORM team located in the IRIT laboratory. Our team is specialized in computer graphics research.

My research focus on real-time rendering and in particular on a physically realistic management of light.

I'm a rendering enthusiast, I like to immerse myself in video games (especially single-player), mainly to discover and be inspired by innovative ideas to approach real-time rendering, not necessarily physically realistic. But also to escape, to discover stories, to make me think, or simply to dream ...

I don't really know why, but I am also a lover of space and especially of astronautics. For me, it is one of the most fascinating science.
