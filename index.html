Pierre Mézières

I am a Computer Science PhD student at the University Paul Sabatier III of Toulouse, supervised by Mathias Paulin, team member of the STORM team located in the IRIT laboratory. Our team is specialized in computer graphics research.

My research focus on real-time rendering and in particular on a physically realistic management of light.
