Pierre Mézières

I am a Computer Science PhD student at the University Paul Sabatier of Toulouse, supervised by Mathias Paulin, team member of the STORM team located in the IRIT laboratory. Our team is specialized in computer graphics research.

My research focus on real-time rendering and in particular on an efficient use of the spherical harmonics.

Rogue (Render Object Graphic Useless Engine)
This 3D engine is oriented for fast real-time rendering prototyping. The last public version made was released before I started my PhD thesis, the vast majority of my thesis work is coded on it. Shortly after the end of my PhD, the public version of Rogue will be updated with all the improvements I coded during the 3 years of my thesis.
